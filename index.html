My Recipies

Below I have given my 3 icnonic recipies. I have become quite famous in my house with these mouth watering omlete recipies. You should give it a try too!

Egg Half Fry

First you have to take an egg. Then just crack it on fry pan. There should be a little portion of oil in fry pan. Add some salt to your taste. Fry it for about a couple of minutes. Your Egg Half Fry is now ready to eat!

Omlette Without Topings

First you have to take an egg. Then crack it in a bowl. Mix it with your favourite kitchen weapon. Add some salt to the mixture. Then pour it in fry pan. After a couple of minutes you can have it without any complaints!

Omlette With Topings

First you have to take an egg. Then crack it in a bowl. Add some salt to the mixture.Add veggies you like(example:tomato,onion). Mix it with your favourite kitchen weapon. Then pour it in fry pan. Your Omlette is now ready to go in your belly!
